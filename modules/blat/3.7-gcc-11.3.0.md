# BLAT 3.7  
(bio)  
  
  
BLAT on DNA is designed to quickly find sequences of 95% and
greater similarity of length 25 bases or more.  
  
  
### Toolchain  
{'name': 'GCC', 'version': '11.3.0'}  
  
### Toolchain Options
