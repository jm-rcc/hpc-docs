# BCFtools 1.18  
(bio)  
  
  
Samtools is a suite of programs for interacting with high-throughput sequencing data.
 BCFtools - Reading/writing BCF2/VCF/gVCF files and calling/filtering/summarising SNP and short indel sequence
 variants
